About

The Archimedeans Annual Dinner will be taking place on the 10th of March at the DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel. Enjoy yourself with a drinks reception, where you can mingle with other society members and our honorary guests, followed by a three course meal with wine. Professor Caulfield will be the guest speaker for the night.

Dress code is black tie.

Honorary Guests

Each year we take your suggestions as to who we should invite as our honorary guests. This is done as part of the ticketing system. Please suggest your favourite lecturers, supervisors, or any other members of the Maths department that we should invite and we’ll try and organise it so that they sit close to you.
